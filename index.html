Why do I code?

I have always been equally interested in both art and logic. I'm left-handed, and I used to think that automatically made me "right-brained", meaning I favored creativity and art over logic and patterns. But there's no way it's that simple; I do love painting and photography, but I love Sudoku and crossword puzzles just as much. Coding provides me with infinite tools to express myself and challenge myself both creatively and intellectually. It's the best of both worlds!
